How It Works

Create a Plan

Brunch, hiking, game night, a weekend trip — set the details in seconds and invite exactly who you want.

Invite Your Crew

Pick friends directly or share with a group. Only the people you choose can see it. No strangers, no algorithms.

Coordinate and Go

Every plan has its own group chat. Work out the details, confirm the vibe, and show up.

Discover What's Nearby

See what your friends are up to and find activities happening around you — organized by category.
