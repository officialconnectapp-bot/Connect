Connect — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

Introduction

Connect ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Please read this policy carefully. By using Connect, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect different types of information to provide and improve the Connect experience.

Information You Provide Directly

  • Names & Emails: When you create an account, we collect your name and email address for authentication, account management, and communication purposes.
  • Phone Numbers: You may optionally provide your phone number to help friends find and connect with you within the app.
  • Profile Pictures: We collect photos you upload via your device's camera or photo library to serve as your profile picture. These are stored on our servers via Firebase Storage.
  • Location Data: When you grant permission, we collect your foreground location data to help you discover nearby events and show your location to friends within the app. We only collect location when the app is actively in use.
  • User-Generated Content: We collect event descriptions, group chat messages, comments, and other content you create and share within the app.
  • Friend Connections: We maintain a record of your friend connections and social graph to enable social features and recommendations.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Push Notification Tokens: We collect unique push notification tokens from your device to send you alerts about events, messages, and friend activities.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your device type, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers for troubleshooting and service improvement.
  • Usage Data: We log information about how you interact with the app, including features used, content viewed, and event participation, to understand user behavior and improve the app.
  • IP Address & Network Data: We may collect your IP address and network information for security, fraud prevention, and analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To create and manage your account, authenticate you, and provide the core features of Connect.
  • Personalization: To customize your experience, show you relevant nearby events, and deliver personalized content.
  • Communication: To send you push notifications about friend activity, event updates, messages, and app-related announcements.
  • Social Features: To enable friend connections, group chats, event invitations, and other social interactions.
  • Service Improvement: To analyze user behavior, identify trends, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Safety & Security: To prevent abuse, detect fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, and protect user safety.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal requests.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Information like your name, profile picture, location (when nearby), and user-generated content may be visible to other Connect users based on your privacy settings and the features you use.
  • Service Providers: We share necessary information with Firebase (Google Cloud) for authentication, data storage, and analytics. Firebase is our primary backend infrastructure provider.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests from authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Safety Concerns: We may share information to prevent harm, enforce our Terms of Service, or protect the rights and safety of Connect, our users, or the public.

We ensure that any third-party service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as necessary to provide services to Connect and to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

Data Storage & Security

Data Storage: Your information is stored on Firebase infrastructure operated by Google Cloud. By using Connect, you acknowledge that your data is processed and stored in the United States and other jurisdictions where Firebase services are available.

Security Measures: We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Authentication
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention: We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data as described in the Account Deletion section below.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to access and review your personal information. You can view much of this information directly in your Connect account settings.
  • Correction: You may update or correct your name, email, phone number, and profile picture at any time through your account settings.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions for legal compliance and legitimate business purposes.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving push notifications through your device settings or app preferences.
  • Location Controls: You can disable location sharing at any time through your device settings or app permissions. This will prevent foreground location collection.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the contact information provided below.

Account Deletion

You may request deletion of your Connect account at any time by contacting us at official.connectapp@gmail.com. When you delete your account:

  • Your account will be deactivated and removed from the app
  • Your personal information will be deleted from our active systems within 30 days
  • User-generated content (event descriptions, chat messages) may remain visible in the app if created in shared spaces, but will be anonymized or attributed to a deleted account
  • We may retain certain information for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or legitimate business purposes as permitted by law

Children's Privacy

Connect is designed for users 13 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's account. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at official.connectapp@gmail.com.

Location Data

Foreground Location Only: Connect only collects location data while the app is in active use (in the foreground). We do not collect background location data. This data is used to:

  • Show you nearby events and activities
  • Display your approximate location to friends (if you have chosen to share it)
  • Improve event discovery and recommendations

Your Control: You can disable location sharing at any time through your device's location settings or through the app's permission settings. Disabling location will limit your ability to discover nearby events but will not affect other app features.

Privacy Note: Your precise location is not stored on our servers. We only use your location to provide nearby recommendations and then discard the precise coordinates.

Push Notifications

Connect uses push notifications to notify you about friend activity, event updates, messages, and app announcements. We collect device push notification tokens to send these notifications.

Managing Notifications: You can control push notifications by:

  • Adjusting notification settings within the Connect app
  • Disabling notifications in your device's system settings
  • Opting out of specific notification categories in your app preferences

GDPR & CCPA Compliance

GDPR (European Users): If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and the right to withdraw consent. We process personal information based on your consent when you create an account and use the app. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

CCPA (California Users): If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt-out of sales (though we do not sell personal information). To exercise these rights, contact us at official.connectapp@gmail.com.
